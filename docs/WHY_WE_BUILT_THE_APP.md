# Why We Built the App — What It’s For

**Dogan Consult** — ICT Engineering Services (www.doganconsult.com)

---

## 1. Purpose (why we built it)

The app is **Dogan Consult’s digital platform** for:

1. **Company presence** — Show who Dogan Consult is: independent engineering consulting in **ICT infrastructure and telecommunications**, focused on **KSA and GCC**, with **critical digital infrastructure** and compliance (e.g. NCA, CITC).
2. **Lead generation** — Capture **inquiries and project requests** from the website (contact/inquiry forms) and feed them into a backend so the team can qualify and work them.
3. **Customer & partner access** — Give **customers** a place to see their inquiries, projects, and account (**customer portal / workspace**), and give **partners** a place to submit leads, see commissions, and pipeline (**partner portal**).
4. **Internal team** — Give the **full Dogan Consult organization** one place to **work together**: manage leads, pipeline, engagements, commissions, and operations — so the team can **serve customer needs** and **serve partner business** effectively.

So: we built it to **run the business online** (brand, leads, portals) and to let the **whole organization work together** to serve customers and partners.

---

## 2. Who it’s for (users)

| User | Use |
|------|-----|
| **Visitors (public)** | See services, trust/compliance, submit inquiry, track inquiry, read legal/terms. |
| **Customers** | Log in, see workspace (overview, pipeline, inquiries, tenders, demos, projects, contracts, messages, settings). |
| **Partners** | Register, log in (API key), submit leads, view dashboard (commissions, pipeline, activity, analytics, resources, training, settings). |
| **Internal team (Dogan Consult)** | The full organization: log in, work together on leads, partners, pipeline, engagements, commissions, gates, analytics, audit, files, mail, team, ERP, AI assistant, settings — to serve customer needs and partner business. |

---

## 3. What it’s used for (use cases)

- **Marketing & brand** — Bilingual (EN/AR) landing: hero, services, why choose us, contact, trust/compliance.
- **Lead capture** — Inquiry form, thanks page, track inquiry; leads stored in DB for the internal team to qualify and work.
- **Customer self-service** — One place for “my” inquiries, projects, pipeline, messages, account (workspace).
- **Partner channel** — Partners register, submit leads, see commissions and pipeline; internal team approves and serves partner business.
- **Internal team collaboration** — Full organization: leads, pipeline, engagements, commissions, gates, analytics, audit, files, mail, team, settings — working together to serve customers and partners.
- **Compliance / trust** — Content and badges around NCA, CITC, ISO, KSA data residency, etc., to support sales and trust.

---

## 4. In one sentence

**We built the app so Dogan Consult’s full organization can work together on one platform: to serve customer needs and to serve partner business — with a public website, customer portal, partner portal, and one place for the internal team to collaborate.**  
**Use:** public website + customer portal + partner portal + internal team (full organization).
